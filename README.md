# Where Are You - Privacy Policy

## Effective Date
February 17, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to the "Where Are You" app (hereinafter referred to as "this app"). We take your privacy protection and personal information security very seriously. This privacy policy aims to explain how this app collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information, as well as the rights you enjoy.

## Data Collection

This app is a locally stored application. All data is stored locally on your device and will not be uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

### Types of Data Collected

- **User-created content**: Information such as rooms, furniture, and items you create while using this app is only stored locally on your device
- **Image data**: Room background images, item images you upload, etc., are only stored locally on your device

## Data Usage

This app only uses the data you create locally on your device for:

- Displaying information about rooms, furniture, and items
- Providing search functionality
- Supporting data addition, editing, and deletion operations

## Data Storage and Protection

- **Storage method**: All data is stored locally on your device using iOS system's standard storage mechanisms
- **Data protection**: Since data is only stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security status of your device
- **Data backup**: If you have enabled iCloud backup, app data may be backed up through iCloud, depending on your iCloud settings

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, including but not limited to:
- Analytics services
- Advertising services
- Social media integration
- Cloud storage services

## User Rights

As a user, you enjoy the following rights:

- **Access right**: You can view and access all data stored in this app at any time
- **Modification right**: You can modify or update the data you created at any time
- **Deletion right**: You can delete the data you created at any time
- **Data export**: You can export app data through your device's backup function

## Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you within the app or through other appropriate means.
